AHIMSA

Ahimsa means non violence or peace , do you remember our national flag- Orange,White,Green
  • ORANGE refers to sacrifice
  • WHITE refers to peace
  • GREEN refers to prosperity
So it is a crucial part to maintain peace within everyone and see what is good in then instead of seeing their downsides The father of the Indian nation Mahatma Gandhi himself was a great follower of Ahimsa. Who wanted to get freedom without the loss of lifes. Hence it is important for us to practice peace everywhere we go and always be peaceful with others
